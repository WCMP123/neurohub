How Medical Aid Works Here

We will submit your medical aid claims on your behalf. Every claim includes a diagnostic code (ICD-10 code) as required by your scheme.

⚠️ Important: Before starting treatment, please check with your medical aid what psychology benefits you have — including limits, how much they pay per session, and how much of your savings account remains.

What we do

Submit claims to your medical aid on your behalf. Let us know if you'd prefer we don't submit.

What you're responsible for

Any shortfall — the difference between what your medical aid pays and our full rate — remains your responsibility.

Why shortfalls happen

Medical aids pay according to their own rates, which may be lower than our practice rates.

If your claim is declined

Medical aids can decline claims they consider not medically necessary. The full session fee is still owed.

Prescribed Minimum Benefits (PMB)

PMBs cover certain serious conditions. For mental health, your entitlement per year is:

Hospital admission

Up to 21 days per year for in-patient mental health treatment.

Out-patient sessions

Up to 15 psychotherapy sessions per year if not admitted.

🔗 These two benefits are combined. Days in hospital reduce how many out-patient sessions remain.
Example 1
Admitted for 5 days → you have 11 out-patient sessions remaining for the year.
Example 2
Used 5 out-patient sessions, then admitted for 10 days → 2 sessions remaining for the year.
🔒 Privacy note: Applying for PMB requires sharing your diagnosis with your medical aid. This could affect life insurance, immigration, and career applications. Please consider this carefully before applying.

It is your responsibility as the main member to track how many PMB days and sessions you have used.

Payments

30
Pay within 30 days of your session Accounts must be settled within 30 days of treatment date. If you need more time, please contact us before the deadline.
30+
Interest charged after 30 days Interest will be added to any outstanding balance after the 30-day period.
90
Possible debt collection after 90 days Accounts unpaid after 90 days may be handed to a collection agency. We will always try to reach you directly first.

How to pay: Electronic transfer (EFT) using bank details on your statement, or by card at our offices. Please include your account number for EFT payments.

We do not accept cash payments.
